Introduction

Data Scientist & Economist

I am L. Maharaj — a data scientist and economist with a focus on quantitative research at the intersection of economic systems and sociological behavior. My work is driven by a conviction that data, rigorously applied, can illuminate the structural forces that shape societies, markets, and institutions.

With a background spanning statistical modeling, econometrics, and applied machine learning, I build analytical frameworks designed not merely to describe — but to explain, and where possible, to anticipate.

"The world runs on patterns. My work is finding them."

Research Philosophy

How I Approach the Work

Every project begins with a question worth asking. I am drawn to the spaces where economic data and sociological narrative converge — where the numbers alone are insufficient, and context becomes as important as the coefficient.

My research spans labor economics, capital markets, social stratification, and energy systems. The common thread is a belief that the most meaningful insights live at the boundary between disciplines, and that quantitative methods are most powerful when guided by a deep understanding of the human systems they describe.

All work presented on this site is entirely personal and independent — pursued out of intellectual curiosity and a commitment to honest inquiry, unaffiliated with any institution, agency, or employer.
